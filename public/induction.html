Topic 2 — Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

All contractors, exhibitors and suppliers must wear appropriate PPE at all times during Build-Up and Breakdown. Minimum requirements include: safety boots (steel toe-capped), hard hat/helmet, high-visibility vest, and any additional PPE required for your specific trade.

⚠ Access to the venue will be denied without proper PPE.

Topic 3 — Housekeeping & Waste Management

All contractors and exhibitors are responsible for maintaining a clean and safe working environment. This includes: removing all waste daily, keeping walkways and emergency exits clear at all times, disposing of hazardous materials correctly, and ensuring no trip or slip hazards are left unattended.

✓ I will maintain a clean working area and dispose of waste correctly
Waste removal is the venue's responsibility
Topic 4 — Emergency Procedures

In the event of an emergency: immediately stop all work, raise the alarm, evacuate to the designated assembly point, do not use lifts, do not re-enter the building until cleared by emergency services, and report all incidents to the Safety Manager immediately.

Assembly Point: As indicated on site signage

✓ I understand emergency procedures and know my assembly point
I should continue working until told to stop
Topic 5 — Power Tools & Equipment

All power tools and electrical equipment must be tested and tagged before use. Operators must be trained and competent. Extension cords must not present trip hazards. No tools may be left unattended while powered. Generators require prior written approval.

Any person may use power tools without training
✓ Only trained, competent operators may use power tools and all equipment must be tested and tagged
Topic 6 — Vehicles & Material Handling

Vehicle access to the venue is restricted to designated times and areas only. Speed limit on site is 10km/h. Forklifts and pallet jacks require a valid operator certificate. All loading and unloading must be supervised. Pedestrians have right of way at all times.

✓ I will comply with all vehicle and material handling regulations on site
I can park and drive anywhere on the venue property
Topic 7 — Fire Safety

No open flames are permitted in the venue without written approval from the Safety Manager. All hot work requires a hot work permit. Fire extinguishers must not be obstructed. Smoking is only permitted in designated areas. Report any fire hazards immediately.

I can smoke anywhere in the venue
✓ I understand fire safety rules and will only smoke in designated areas
